My favourite TV Show : Strip-Tease

Strip-Tease is a Belgian television documentary program that first aired on RTBF 1 in 1985. It later aired in Franco-Belgian on France 3 in 1992. According to its creators, it aims to treat subjects "caught in the facts of society".[1] The program is considered controversial for its crude discussion of difficult situations.

My favourite book : As I Lay Dying (William Faulkner)

As I Lay Dying is a 1930 novel, in the genre of Southern Gothic,[1] by American author William Faulkner. Faulkner said that he wrote the novel from midnight to 4:00 AM over the course of six weeks and that he did not change a word of it.[2] Faulkner wrote it while working at a power plant, published it in 1930, and described it as a "tour de force".
